Professional

I graduated from Polytechnic University of Aguascalientes with a B.Eng. in Mechatronics. I received the M.S. in Robotics and Advanced Manufacturing from CINVESTAV under the supervision of the professors Vicente Parra and Fransico Ruiz. Since 2012 I am with the Robotics and Automation group of Research Center E Piaggio of the Engineering School Leonardo da Vinci at University of Pisa where I am currently working toward the Ph.D degree. My Advisor is the professor Antonio Bicchi and my main research topic is constrained motion planning and execution for soft robots.
Before starting my master studies I was working first as a Technical Support Engineer in Trucka, and then in Gp Electromecanica as an Automation Engineer. Once I got my M.S. degree I worked for the technology center of Whirlpool Corporation as Engineer analyst, my job was to perform Multibody Dinamic Simulations. After these experience on industry I decided to follow one of my dreams and try to become a Ph.D. I am in the way.

Personal

My full name is José Manuel Bonilla Jiménez, I was born in Aguascalientes, Mexico in 1985. I am married to Vanessa Sánchez whom I have three children with: Vanessa is 10 years old, Paulette is 8 years old and Manuel is 2 years old.
I grew up in Aguascalientes, in 2011 I moved to Saltillo to continue my studies and 2 years later to Monterrey to work. Now I am living in Pisa where I'm currently pursuing a PhD in Robotics.
